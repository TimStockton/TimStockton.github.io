Research (Machine Learning & Data Analysis)

I co-authored a published research paper providing a comparative analysis of machine learning preprocessing techniques, including feature selection and feature extraction, and their impact on different machine learning models. We used a breast cancer dataset and Python (pandas, numpy, seaborn, matplotlib, sklearn), applying the CFS, RFE, LDA, PCA preprocessing techniques then training logistic regression, decision tree, random forest, support vector machine, neural network, and XGBoost tree models.

I provide free access to the research paper here.

It's published here as part of Lecture Notes on Data Engineering and Communications Technologies (LNDECT,volume 201).


I independantly researched the ratio between population and internet users by country, and investigated it's correlation with social and economic factors. I used Python (pandas, seaborn) for this, and created a data analysis poster breaking down my findings. Interesting data points and relationships of statistics like GDP, life expectancy, social support, corruption, and freedom are discussed with supporting visuals.

I provide free access to the poster here.
